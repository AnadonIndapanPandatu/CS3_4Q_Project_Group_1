History of this Advocacy Group

This advocacy group arose from the passions of
its founders, Alan Clarence Anadon, Simone Izadore
Indapan and Tatiana Margaret Pandatu.
These passions root from the reason of wanting to
prevent the earth's decline which is neglected
despite the clear signs of climate change and global
warming. Trees play a role in absorbing Carbon
Dioxide in the athmosphere hence this advocacy's
mission to create a greener environment through
one tree at a time. In this advocacy, we create
friendships along with a healthier planet.

Planting trees will help our planet!
